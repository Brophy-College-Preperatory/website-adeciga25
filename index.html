What is Pokémon?

Pokémon, short for "Pocket Monsters," is a popular media franchise created by Satoshi Tajiri and Ken Sugimori, first introduced in 1996 by Nintendo, Game Freak, and Creatures. The franchise revolves around fictional creatures called Pokémon, which humans, known as Pokémon Trainers, capture and train to battle each other. Players explore a world filled with diverse Pokémon species, each with unique abilities and traits. The central goal in the games is often to capture, train, and battle with Pokémon, aiming to become the Pokémon Champion.

Over time, Pokémon expanded into video games, an animated TV series, movies, trading card games, toys, and other merchandise, becoming a global cultural phenomenon. At its heart, Pokémon is about adventure, strategy, friendship, and discovery, resonating with people of all ages.

Main Line Games

The mainline Pokémon games are a beloved series of role-playing games that invite players to assume the role of Pokémon Trainers, capturing and training various creatures to battle one another. Launched in 1996, the franchise has evolved through multiple generations, each introducing new Pokémon, regions, and gameplay mechanics, such as Gyms, online battling, and open-world exploration. Players embark on adventures filled with quests, rivalries, and the goal of becoming a Pokémon Champion, while also engaging in trading and battling with others. This combination of exploration, strategy, and social interaction has made Pokémon a cultural phenomenon, resonating with fans of all ages around the world.

TCG

The Pokémon Trading Card Game (TCG) is a collectible card game that invites players to build decks featuring Pokémon, Trainer cards, and Energy cards to engage in strategic battles against one another. Since its launch in 1996, the TCG has expanded through numerous sets and expansions that introduce new mechanics and Pokémon, keeping gameplay fresh and exciting. Players take turns drawing cards and using their strategies to knock out opponents' Pokémon and collect Prize cards, all while fostering a competitive scene that includes local tournaments and the prestigious Pokémon World Championships. With its blend of strategy, collectibility, and nostalgia, the Pokémon TCG remains a beloved part of the Pokémon franchise, captivating fans of all ages.
